Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our startup programs and mentorship opportunities.

Who can apply to the accelerator program?

Our accelerator program is designed for early-stage startups with a minimum viable product (MVP) and some initial traction. We focus on technology-driven startups with the potential for social impact.

Is there a cost to participate in your programs?

Most of our programs are offered at no cost to participants. For some specialized programs, there may be a nominal fee to cover operational costs. Financial assistance is available for startups that demonstrate need.

How are mentors matched with startups?

We match mentors and startups based on industry expertise, specific needs, and mutual interests. Our goal is to create meaningful mentorship relationships that provide value to both parties.

Can I participate remotely?

Yes, all of our programs are designed to be accessible remotely. We use digital tools and platforms to facilitate mentorship, workshops, and community engagement, making our programs accessible to entrepreneurs worldwide.
