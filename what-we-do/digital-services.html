Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our digital services.

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website can be completed in 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects may take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase of your project.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your digital solution remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our support packages include regular updates, security patches, bug fixes, and technical support to address any issues that may arise.

What is your approach to responsive design?

We design all our websites and applications with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they function seamlessly across all devices and screen sizes. Our responsive designs automatically adjust to provide optimal user experiences on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Do you work with specific industries or sectors?

While we have expertise across various sectors, we specialize in working with social enterprises, nonprofits, healthcare organizations, educational institutions, and businesses focused on social impact. Our team understands the unique challenges and opportunities in these sectors.
