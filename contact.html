Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and how we work.

How can I partner with Humanics Lab?

There are multiple ways to partner with us, from collaborating on research projects to engaging our digital services. Contact us through the form above to discuss partnership opportunities that align with your goals.

Do you offer services for startups?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support for startups through our entrepreneurship programs, including mentorship, resources, and access to our network. Visit our Startups & Mentors page to learn more.

How can universities collaborate with Humanics Lab?

We work with universities to establish innovation hubs, support student entrepreneurship, and collaborate on research projects. Contact us to explore potential collaboration opportunities.

What areas do your research initiatives focus on?

Our research initiatives focus on healthcare, education, environmental sustainability, and economic development, with a particular emphasis on leveraging technology for social impact.
