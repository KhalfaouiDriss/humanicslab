Internship Program

Gain valuable experience and make a real impact through our internship opportunities. Work on meaningful projects alongside our team of experts.

Program Overview

Our internship program is designed to provide hands-on experience in technology and social impact. Interns work on real projects that contribute to our mission, gaining valuable skills and making meaningful contributions to communities around the world.

Duration

3-6 months, with flexible start dates throughout the year

Time Commitment

Full-time and part-time options available

Eligibility

Current students or recent graduates with relevant skills and interests

Location

Remote, hybrid, and in-person options in London, UK

Internship Areas

We offer internships in various areas, allowing you to gain experience in your field of interest while contributing to our mission.

Software Development

Work on real-world projects using modern technologies like React, Node.js, and cloud services.

UX/UI Design

Create user-centered designs and interfaces for digital products and services.

Data Science & AI

Analyze data and develop AI solutions to address social challenges.

Digital Marketing

Develop and implement digital marketing strategies to amplify our impact.

Project Management

Coordinate projects and ensure successful delivery of solutions.

Research

Conduct research on emerging technologies and their potential for social impact.

What You'll Gain

Our internship program is designed to provide you with valuable skills, experience, and connections.

Hands-on Experience

Work on real projects that contribute to our mission and impact, gaining practical experience in your field.

Mentorship

Receive guidance and support from experienced professionals who are passionate about your development.

Skill Development

Develop technical and professional skills through structured learning opportunities and hands-on project work.

Career Advancement

Build your portfolio, expand your professional network, and potentially transition to full-time roles.

Application Process

We're looking for passionate individuals who are eager to learn and contribute to our mission.

1

Submit Your Application

Send your CV and a brief cover letter explaining your interests and how you'd like to contribute to our mission.

humanicslab.pro
2

Interview

3

Project Assignment

Based on your skills and interests, we'll match you with a project and team that aligns with your goals and our needs.

4

Onboarding

Once accepted, you'll go through our onboarding process to get familiar with our team, tools, and processes.
