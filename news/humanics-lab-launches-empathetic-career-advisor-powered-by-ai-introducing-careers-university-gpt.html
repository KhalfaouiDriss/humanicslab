Humanics Lab Launches Empathetic Career Advisor Powered by AI: Introducing Careers University GPT!

We’re thrilled to announce the launch of Careers University GPT, an innovative AI-powered chatbot designed to be your empathic and insightful careers advisor.

January 8, 2023
innovationsocial-impact
By Adams Alhassan

Navigating the career landscape can feel like traversing a dense jungle – exciting, full of possibilities, but also potentially overwhelming. At Humanics Lab, we believe everyone deserves supportive and personalised guidance on their career journey. That’s why we’re thrilled to announce the launch of Careers University GPT, an innovative AI-powered chatbot designed to be your empathic and insightful careers advisor.

Meet Careers University GPT:
- Built by Humanics Lab co-founder and seasoned careers advisor Adams Alhassan, Careers University GPT combines Adams’ expert knowledge with the power of Openai GPT-4 technology.
- Personalised for you: Forget generic advice. Careers University GPT delves into your unique aspirations, skills, and interests to develop a personalised roadmap for your career success.
- Detailed and forward-looking: Careers University GPT goes beyond simply suggesting job titles. It analyses future trends and emerging markets to help you understand where your skills will be most valuable in the years to come.
- Empathetic and supportive: Unlike cold algorithms, Careers University GPT understands the emotions and anxieties that come with career choices. It offers encouragement, guidance, and practical tips to navigate difficult decisions.

Careers University GPT is here to help you:

- Explore career options: Discover hidden paths you never knew existed, and gain insights into potential challenges and rewards of different fields.
- Craft your resume and cover letter: Get personalised tips and feedback on your application materials to stand out from the crowd.
- Prepare for interviews: Practice your answers to common questions and refine your skills to make a lasting impression.
- Develop essential skills: Identify areas where you can gain valuable knowledge and experience to boost your employability.

Humanics Lab is committed to using technology for good, and Careers University GPT is a testament to that mission. We believe everyone deserves access to accessible, inclusive, and empathetic career guidance. Whether you’re a fresh graduate or a seasoned professional seeking a career change, University GPT is your AI-powered companion on the path to achieving your career goals.

Ready to embark on your journey? Follow the link to interact with Careers University GPT and take control of your future!

(https://chat.openai.com/g/g-rOld7OMDE-careers-university)
